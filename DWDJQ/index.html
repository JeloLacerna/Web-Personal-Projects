2013

March 2013

This year is when Jelo graduated from highschool!

Hooray!

He is very much excited to go to University!

June 2013

Jelo passed the entrace examination to a decently famous university.

He is planning to take an Engineering program to the said university.

He is now starting a new phase of his life he will never forget.

2018

August 2013 - April 2018

Jelo is enjoying his university life being a Chemical Engineering student

But an important event that would change his life is coming.

His mother said that they would migrate to Canada soon.

July 2018

The day has come. Their flight to Canada is near but Jelo still hasn't finished his studies.

So he would go back after a month to continue and finish his degree.

Time to enjoy the remaining uni days!

2019

July 2019

After a few months, Jelo is finally starting his internship in a sugar milling plant.

He has come across a lot of processing plants during his studies but this is unique.

This is the first time he has entered a food processing complex.

August 2019

Jelo is finally graduating!

Yahoo!

After hundreds of all-nighters, countless cups of coffee, and bottomless amounts of alchohol, blood, sweat and tears.

After 6.5 years, he has done it.

One more challenge is left, the licensure examination!

November 2019

The licensure examination only happens twice a year. Fresh graduates and multiple repeat takers are welcome to take the exam.

This is the final challenge. The final step to be able to officially call himself an Engineer.

Spoiler: He passed the test. And Jelo is now a full-blown engineer.

Celebrations are in order! Woot! Woot!

2021

October 2020

All was going well.

Unfortunately, the Pandemic happened.

So after getting his license, Jelo got stuck in the Philippines for a while.

But he got his chance to go back to Canada on October, so he grabbed it.

He tried to find a job for almost a year. But he is unable to find a job for his degree there.

August 2021

He has finally decided to pursue another career: as a developer.

Jelo enrolled for a web and mobile development program.

He is currently in the said program.

And, he is writing this web app as a requirement for a course.

Ehe! :)
