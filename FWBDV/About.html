About

Jelouie Lacerna

Just some random guy trying to learn how to code.

Hi, I’m Jelouie, or as my family and friends affectionately call me, Jelo. As far as I'm concerned, I'm the only one in the Philippines, or maybe in the world, with that name. Crazy right?

I grew up curious of how things work. I would wonder how a remote-controlled toy car get's the input I give it using the controls. How a TV would show a moving picture on its big screen. How people could communicate using small devices called cellphone over long distances. This curiosity over devices led me to pursue an engineering degree, although not electronics major, but chemical. Why? Because why not. And also, I just like how 'Chemical Engineer' sounds.

After college, I wanted to immediately get a job so I applied to a bunch of companies but world said "F you" to everyone and the pandemic happened. So I decided to migrate to Canada with my family to, hopefully, have better opportunities. My degree isn't really recognized here so I to study again…

…and here I am now, studying to be a developer!
